Pro Tips & Recommendations
📸
Progress Photos Every 2 Weeks
Same lighting, same time (AM fasted), same poses. Weeks 1, 3, 5, 7, 9, 11, 13. The mirror lies — photos don't. This is how you'll see the recomp happening.
⚖️
Weigh Daily, Average Weekly
Daily weight fluctuates. Weigh each morning post-bathroom, but only compare weekly averages. During recomp the scale may barely move while you're visually transforming.
🧪
Bloodwork at Week 7
Get a comprehensive panel mid-protocol — metabolic panel, lipids, fasting glucose, A1C, IGF-1. Add fasting glucose + A1C to confirm MOTS-c is working at the metabolic level. Mid-point check before the 4 mg ramp at week 11.
📅
Thursday Is the Heavy Day
Thursday AM = CJC + MOTS-c + Reta — three injections in a row. Rotate sites: left belly for CJC, right belly for MOTS-c, lower belly for Reta. Plan Thursday as a lighter food day in case Reta nausea hits by afternoon.
⚡
Daily MOTS-c Timing
Take MOTS-c with your AM CJC/Ipa shot, ideally 30–60 min pre-workout on training days. The acute AMPK activation amplifies the workout's metabolic signal. Daily dosing mimics endogenous mitochondrial signaling rhythms — way smoother than 2x weekly bolus.
🤢
Managing GI Sides at Dose Bumps
When you jump to 2 mg (week 5) and 4 mg (week 11), expect 3–7 days of nausea or fullness — most intense Thu afternoon through weekend. Eat smaller, more frequent meals. Ginger tea or ginger chews help. Don't skip meals — shrink them.
🥤
Protein Insurance Policy
At 4 mg Reta, appetite will be crushed. Keep whey isolate or clear protein on hand always. If you can't eat solid, get 40–50g from a shake. Never let protein drop below 200g.
🧂
Electrolytes Are Non-Negotiable
Reta can cause dehydration. Sodium, potassium, magnesium daily. LMNT, Liquid IV, or salt your water. Keeps pumps full, performance high, prevents cramping.
📋
Key Milestone Check-ins
Week 4: Full stack running 4 weeks — sleep and recovery clearly better. First visible recomp shift.

Week 7: Bloodwork + progress photos. Visual recomp should be undeniable. Mid-point reset.

Week 10: Decision point — push to 4 mg Reta, or stay at 2 mg if results are strong and side effects manageable.

Week 14: Final assessment. Photos, measurements, plan next phase.
🛌
Protect Your Sleep
CJC/Ipa's biggest benefit is the GH pulse during deep sleep. Take your PM shot 15–20 min before bed. Room cold and dark. No screens 30 min prior. This is where the magic happens.
